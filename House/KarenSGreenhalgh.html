Privacy Policy

How we use the information you provide this website:

We will potentially use this information to send you news and information about the campaign. When you volunteer online we may ask you to give us contact information and geographic information. We may use this information to send you material about the campaign or to contact you regarding your volunteer status.

In addition, the Federal Election Commission (FEC) and local law may require us to collect personal information from donors.

Information on Children:

This website website does not ask children 13 or under for any information that can be used to identify the child. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children 13 or under. Persons under the age of 18 and under are not permitted to contribute via the website.

Linking to other sites:

Our site links to a limited number of other web sites. This site is not responsible for the content or the privacy policies of these web sites. We are not responsible for the actions of any service providers or other third parties, nor are we responsible for any additional information you provide directly to any third parties. We encourage you to become familiar with their privacy practices before disclosing information directly to any such parties or web sites.

Protecting Your Information

This website has security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of the information under our control. Our server is located in a locked, secure environment.

Receiving Campaign Updates

We send e-mail subscribers periodic news updates about the campaign. If you do not wish to receive updates via e-mail or other communications from the campaign, you can remove your name from our mailing list at any time by unsubscribing from our e-mail list on this site.

Opting Out and Modifying Information

If you need to change your e-mail address, you can unsubscribe from our mailing lists and sign up again using your new information.

Contacting us about privacy:

If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our web site periodically or contact us directly.

We may choose to update this online privacy policy in the future. Please check this page for any updates.
